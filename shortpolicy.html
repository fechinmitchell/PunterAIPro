Privacy Notice

The privacy of our website visitors and clients is very important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding it. In offering an international service we endeavour to adhere to applicable data protection regulations recognising that these vary between countries and regions by following the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) in place in the Europeran Union.

Any of your personal details disclosed when using this web site or communicating with us will be kept safe and will be used for our interactions with you. Our web site uses cookies but these can be blocked using your browser options if you prefer to avoid them.

All coaching sessions are confidential between client and coach in line with the EMCC Code of Ethics. Data on our clients is also maintained in accordance with the EMCC ethical guidelines. Full details on our Privacy Policy can be read in the attached Privacy Notice.
