Welcome to my Psychotherapy and Coaching Practise

I have been working in my private psychotherapy and coaching practice in Ireland for over twenty years giving me deep diverse experience of working with issues in living .We all face difficulties and challenges. Sometimes these stem from stressful situations, problematic relationships or traumatic events and can lead to thoughts and feelings that are confusing or upsetting. I believe therapy is not a ‘one size fits all” process, we are all unique and it is important to me as therapist and coach that I meet you where you are at. Both therapy and coaching are a collaborative process, and I am committed to providing you with therapy and coaching that is safe, confidential, and non-judgmental.

As an accredited member of IAHIP I follow their principles and ethical guidelines. My core training as a therapist is Humanistic and Integrative, and my Coaching Masters is in the existential approach.

Therapy

The word therapy covers talking therapies such as psychotherapy and counselling. Whether you are coming to therapy with a particular issue, a general sense of unwellness, or a deeper search for purpose and meaning therapy offers a safe, confidential place to talk to a trained professional about your feelings and concerns.. Therapy can help you to see things from a different perspective, break free from old unhelpful patterns, heal past traumas, increase self-confidence.

I have a deep understanding of the challenges in life that we can sometimes face and have worked both in voluntary and the private sector, including addiction services, rape crisis services, EAP services. Working together we can help you find an in-depth understanding of yourself, of who you are and help you to resolve internal conflicts. I am passionate about my work and my belief of self-determination and responsibility.

I welcome and value people’s uniqueness, diversity, and autonomy. As an Integrative therapist, I draw from several therapeutic frameworks to meet your unique needs. Life coaching, an existential, holistic approach.

You may be feeling stuck in one area of your life while other areas are flourishing. You may have realized that you are ready for that change you have been thinking about for some time . I can help you identify goals and stumbling blocks that might be stopping you from reaching your desires, possibilities and potential. The process of transition and change can be complex and at these times we can feel anxious and doubt our decision making process .

There are times particularly if we are over worked and running out of steam that our inner critic can niggle away at our resilience and self-confidence leading to self-doubt. My aim as your coach is to help you connect to your motivation , passions and potential as you make your choices . Asking questions so you come up with the answers that will help guide you in your life.
