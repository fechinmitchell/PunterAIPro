My Approach

My psychotherapy and coaching approach, whilst underpinned by theory and research, is warm and non-judgmental. I believe that humor and creativity are core aspects of successful therapy and coaching. I journey with my clients as they develop a deeper understanding of themselves. Helping them to address issues that are getting in the way of their desire to live with more purpose and meaning. My practice is underpinned by a philosophy that believes wisdom and knowledge can be achieved through self-examination.

Both therapy and coaching are a collaborative process, and I am committed to providing you with a therapy and coaching space that is safe, confidential, and non-judgmental.
